Why most decaf tastes like dishwater

Mouldy, stale beans

Because decaf is just a menu item for most coffee companies, they often use leftover beans languishing away in the damp corner of a warehouse. It's an easy way to squeeze more profit.

Washed in Methylene Chloride

The cheapest way to extract caffeine is to soak the beans in chemical solvents. This is nasty stuff you don't want anywhere near your body.

Over-roasted to hide it's sins

It's then heavily roasted to a dark, burnt crisp to cover up any residual chemical taste or dampness. Any flavor that did exist is a distant memory along with it.

Why Decaf?

Jitter Free

Sometimes you want the coffee, not the chaos. Our decaf lets you skip the jitters without missing out on the ritual.

Sleep Like a Baby

Late-night cravings? Early evening wind-down? You can have your cup worry-free and still drift off without counting sheep.

Drink Night & Day

Coffee doesn’t have to come with rules. No cut-off times. No weird chemicals. Just a clean cup, whenever you want it.

Better Heart Health

If you’re here for something easier on the heart, and your health, you’re not alone. Our decaf keeps things calm, clean, and stress-free.

Safe For Pregnancy

Whether you're pregnant, trying, or just playing it safe, our decaf’s a worry-free way to keep the coffee habit going.

Addiction Free

This is drinking coffee on your terms. No need to depend, no pressure to quit. Just delicious enjoyment, one cup at a time.
